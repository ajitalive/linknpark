How it works

Three steps. No friction.

🚗
STEP 1

Stick it on your car

Peel and stick the LinkNPark QR sticker on your windshield or bumper. Register the code in the app.

📱
STEP 2

Someone scans it

A person nearby scans the QR with any phone camera. No app install required on their end.

🔔
STEP 3

You get notified instantly

The report reaches you in real time through the LinkNPark app. Move your car, respond, or escalate.
